GIVE PATIENTS WITH GENOTYPE CYP2C19 THEIR
BEST CHANCE WITH 12 MONTHS BRILINTA1,2

Response to BRILINTA is not affected by patient CYP2C19 genotype.
Compared with BRILINTA, ACS patients with Genotype CYP2C19 on clopidogrel showed a 30.2% relative increase in the primary endpoint, a composite of CV death, MI or stroke at 12 months (11.2% vs 8.6%); p=0.038.

Compared with BRILINTA, ACS patients on clopidogrel have a 19.4% relative increase in the primary endpoint, a composite of CV death, MI or stroke, at 12 months (11.7% vs 9.8%; p < 0.001).1

Compared with clopidogrel, ACS patients on BRILINTA showed reduced risk of recurrent CV events in the overall PLATO group and the Genotype CUP2C19 sub group for up to 12 months.
