Compared with clopidogrel, patients with kidney disease on BRILINTA showed a reduction in relative risk of
CV death, MI or stroke (17.3% vs 22%).

Compared with clopidogrel, patients with chronic kidney disease on BRILINTA showed an increase in relative risk
