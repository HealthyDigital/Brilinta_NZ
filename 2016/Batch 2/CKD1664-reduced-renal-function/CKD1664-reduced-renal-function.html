Increased risk of death
  • The prognosis in patients after a coronary event with
    impaired excretory renal function is significantly worse
    than in a comparable group with normal renal function.1,2

Increased bleeding risk
  • In patients with GFR <60ml/min, the bleeding
    risk is higher in comparison with patients with
    GFR >60ml/min.1
