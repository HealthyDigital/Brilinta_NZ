Ticagrelor (180mg loading dose, 90mg twice daily), in preference to clopidogrel, is recommended in addition to ASA and maintained over 12 months unless there are contraindications such as excessive risk of bleeding if no contraindication. I B

stemi undergoing primary pci class level of evidence
DAPT with a combination of aspirin and ticagrelor is recommended (over aspirin and clopidogrel). I A
DAPT with aspirin must be continued for up to 12 months after STEMI in patients who receive a stent. I C
DAPT should be used for up to 1 year in patients with STEMI who did not receive a stent. IIA C
stemi undergoing primary pci class level of evidence
P2Y12 inhibitor therapy should be given for 1 year to patients with STEMI who receive a stent (BMS or DES) during primary PCI using the following doses:
• ticagrelor: 180mg loading as early as possible, 90mg twice a day for 1 year		 I B
A loading dose of a P2Y12 receptor inhibitor should be given as early as possible or at time of primary PCI to patients with STEMI. I B
