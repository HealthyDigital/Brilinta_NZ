UNLIKE THIENOPYRIDINES, BRILINTA binds
reversibly to the P2Y12 receptor1

  • Ticagrelor binds reversibly and leaves
    the receptor intact
  • When ticagrelor is bound, ADP can still bind,
    but does not induce receptor activation
  • Thienopyridines bind covalently rendering the
    receptor non-functional for the life of the platelet
