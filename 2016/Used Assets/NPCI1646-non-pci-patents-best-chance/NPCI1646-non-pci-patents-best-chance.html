In the overall PLATO study, the primary composite endpoint of reduction in CV death, MI or stroke was met - 9.8% for BRILINTA 90mg and 11.7% for clopidogrel (ARR 1.9%, RRR, 16% HR 0.84; 95% Cl 0.77-0.92, p<0.001)1,2

Compared with BRILINTA, ACS patients on clopidogrel have a 27.5% relative increase in CV death at
12 months (5.1% vs 4%); p=0.001.1
