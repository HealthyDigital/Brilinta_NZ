GIVE ACSCABG PATIENTS THEIR BEST CHANCE WITH 12 MONTHS BRILINTA1,2

One CV death is prevented for every 26 ACS CABG patients treated with BRILINTA instead of clopidogrel for 12 months.

Compared with BRILINTA, ACS patients on clopidogrel have a 27.5% relative increase in CV death at 12 months (5.1% vs 4%); p=0.001.1

Compared with clopidogrel, ACS patients on BRILINTA showed a reduction in relative risk of CV death in the overall PLATO group and the CABG sub-group for up to 12 months.
