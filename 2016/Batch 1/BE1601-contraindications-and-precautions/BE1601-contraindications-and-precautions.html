BRILINTA, in combination with aspirin, is indicated for the prevention of atherothrombotic events in adult patients with Acute Coronary Syndrome (unstable angina or myocardial infarction), including those managed medically or with PCI or CABG1

Contraindications specific to BRILINTA Precautions specific to BRILINTA

  • Hypersensitivity to ticagrelor or to any of
    the excipients
  • History of intracranial haemorrhage
  • Modest-to-severe hepatic impairment
  • Those taking strong CYP3A4 inhibitors such
    as ketoconazole, clarithromycin, nefazodone,
    ritonavir and atazanavir
  • Use with caution if patient has a history
    of asthma and/or COPD
  • Use with caution in patients at risk of
    bradycardic events
  • BRILINTA in patients with uric acid
    nephropathy is discouraged
  • BRILINTA is not recommended in patients
    taking >40mg simvastatin
