Ticagrelor as crushed tablets mixed in water, given orally or administered through a nasogastric tube into the stomach, has a comparable bioavailability to whole tablets
with regards to AUC and Cmax for ticagrelor and the active metabolite. Initial exposure (0.5 and 1 hour post-dose) from crushed ticagrelor tablets mixed in water was
higher compared to whole tablets, with a generally identical concentration profile thereafter (2 to 48 hours). *Even though ticagrelor does not require biotransformation
for activation, it’s important to note that it is actually metabolised into active and inactive metabolite.
