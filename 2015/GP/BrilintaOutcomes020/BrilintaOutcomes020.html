Brilinta saves even more lives than clopidogrel1

  • One life is saved for every 91 ACS patients treated with Brilinta instead of clopidogrel for 12 months1
  • New Zealand Guidelines recommend Brilinta as the preferred antiplatelet in combination with aspirin2,3
  • Brilinta, in combination with aspirin, should be continued for up to 12 months4
  • Major bleeding did not differ between Brilinta and clopidogrel.
    Non-CABG major bleeding was significantly higher with Brilinta than clopidogrel (p=0.03)1,4
  • Minor side effects (including minor bleeding and mild to moderate dyspnoea) with Brilinta are usually manageable and do not generally require discontinuation1,4
