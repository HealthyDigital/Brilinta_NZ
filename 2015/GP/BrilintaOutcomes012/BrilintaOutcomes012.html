non adherence to dual antiplatelet treatment may increase mortality

  • Following an MI, discontinuation of dual antiplatelet treatment during the first 12 months is common, and is associated with an increased risk of death or recurrent MI1,2
  • Older age and female gender are known predictors of decreased adherence to evidence based medicines following acute MI3
