12 MONTHS TREATMENT WITH BRILINTA IS RECOMMENDED1-7

  • Patients who discontinue antiplatelet treatment prematurely are at increased risk for further cardiac events1
  • New Zealand Guidelines recommend treatment with Brilinta should be continued for 12 months for all appropriate patients with Acute Coronary Syndrome2,3
  • Major international guidelines also recommend treatment with Brilinta be continued for 12 months irrespective of type of ACS or treatment strategy4,5,6
  • In the PLATO trial, Brilinta for 12 months significantly reduced the combined risk of CV death, MI or stroke compared to clopidogrel7
